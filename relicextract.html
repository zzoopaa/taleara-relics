Relic Extract

Info

Crafting Material

Crafted from: Relic Present

Unique Colors: Super Relic



Appearance & Effects

In-game description: The colors of the relic present. Recolorable items can be colored to the "Super Relic" color variant at any dyer.

Relic Extract is not a relic, but is a related item that can be crafted from Relic Presents. The crafting recipe for Relic Extract is unlocked when the player gets a Relic Present for the first time. A player can craft 1 Relic Present into 2 Relic Extract. Relic Extract can be applied to any recolorable non-relic rarity gear or accessory to give it the Super Relic paint.


Inspiration

Tid wanted to give players the ability to give normal accessories and any other piece of recolorable gear the Super Relic paint.
