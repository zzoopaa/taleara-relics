Secrets in Taleara

This page is not related to relics in any way, I just wanted to add a page for secrets.

The developer, Tid, has added quite a few secrets to the game, some of which can be viewed or accessed by players and some of which cannot.


Full List

Super Secret Settings

The Super Secret Settings are 'fun' settings only intended for messing around in the game. To enable them, player's can type 'supersecretsettings' in chat, or click the tiny brown square in the top left of the settings menu.

1 Petabyte Zip Bomb

The 1 Petabyte Zip Bomb is a secret item that only developers can spawn into player's inventories. The item itself weighs a hundred billion pounds or some ridiculous number like that. This means it cannot be stored and will permanently hinder the player's movement speed if they attempt to keep it. It also cannot be sold for money. The only way to get rid of it is to drop it on the ground.
