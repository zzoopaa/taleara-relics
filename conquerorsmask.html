Appearance & Effects

In-game description: Happy birthday Bank :)

The Conqueror's Mask appears to resemble a traditional Chinese Nuo mask. It loosely resembles the face of a demon, with horns, protruding tusks, and glowing eyes. The mask itself is black with gold accents. The eyes come in a default color called Xiao Teal, which is unique to the mask. When the mask is recolored, only the glowing eyes change colors.


Inspiration

The Conqueror's Mask was inspired by Xiao's Yaksha Mask from the game Genshin Impact. This is alluded to by the Conquerer's Mask unique color, Xiao Teal. The mask in more loosley based on Chinese Nuo masks, which are traditional masks that resemble demons used to ward off evil spirits in Chinese culture. The Conqueror's Mask used to be the only relic in Taleara that could buff the player, before this buff was removed.
