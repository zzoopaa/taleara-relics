Appearance & Effects

In-game description: spacebubble

Oof's Space Halo is a galaxy that replaces the player's head. It has a central cloud that is made up of deep blacks and purples, with a ring of brighter purples and whites around the outside.


Inspiration

Oof's Space Halo appears to be inspired by a black hole. The center looks like the event horizon of a black hole and the ring is very similar to the accretion disk of material that often appears around black holes.
