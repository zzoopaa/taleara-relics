What Are Relics?

Relics are rare cosmetics in Taleara that can be obtained from opening Relic Presents. Relic Presents can be obtained through many common game actions. The current ways to obtain them are are:

  • 1/1000 from a chest
  • 1/2000 from a mob kill
  • 1/2000 from catching a fish
  • 1/500 from a Voltbolt Chest

There is also a pity system for relics. All of the above actions except for Voltbolt Chests give 1 pity, and once a player reaches 3500 pity, they are garuanteed a relic. Getting a relic at any time will reset pity to 0.

There are currently 25 obtainable relics in the game. Some of them can be recolored while others only have one variant. You can recolor relics at Rowan the Painter for free. Some relics have an 'owner', which is the player who the developer added the relic to the game for, and often the person who had the original idea for it. Many relics are inspired by other games and media. Most relic owners were gifted a copy of their relic without having to open it from presents.
