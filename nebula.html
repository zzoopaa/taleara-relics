Appearance & Effects

In-game description: astral looking aura ooo

The Nebula is a simple aura that appears as a glowing cloud around the player. As the name states, it is intended to look like a nebulous gas cloud of star dust.


Inspiration

The Nebula was inspired by the clouds of gas that stars form from. The dev text of the Nebula states that it was originally going to be a fart cloud, but he didn't want to make players feel bad for getting it, so it was made recolorable. The fart cloud look can still be achieved if the player colors it lime.
