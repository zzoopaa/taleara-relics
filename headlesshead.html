Headless Head

Info

Slot: Accessory

Recolorable?: No

Unique Colors: None

Particle Effects: None

Sound Effects: None

Owner: None



Appearance & Effects

In-game description: Where are your BRAINS??

The Headless Head is an accessory that removes the head from player's character. It has no other effects or traits otherwise. It is an accessory so it can be layered with other head equipments.


Inspiration

The Headless Head was inspired by the Headless Head, an extremely rare cosmetic from Arcane Odyssey. The Headless Head in general is based on the head accessory from the Headless Horseman bundle on Roblox.
