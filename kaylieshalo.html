Kaylie's Halo

Info

Slot: Accessory

Recolorable?: Yes

Unique Colors: Arcane Blue, Arcane Pink

Particle Effects: None

Sound Effects: None

Owner: KaylieSpark



Appearance & Effects

In-game description: It drifts above you effortlessly, as if it chose you long before you found it.

Kaylie's Halo is a simple torus that floats above the player's head. It glows and can be recolored. Lighter colors will make it shine more brightly, while darker colors will mute the glow. Kaylie's Halo has two unique colors, Arcane Pink and Arcane Blue.


Inspiration

Kaylie's Halo has no particular inspiration behind it. The owner, KaylieSpark, wanted a relic that would pair well with a lot of outfits.
