Appearance & Effects

In-game description: Listening for bits and quirks

The Black Witch's Ears appear to be a pair of oversized bat ears. They are adorned with golden ear cuffs and white fluff. The Black Witch's Ears also come with a nose ring that appears on the player's face. The outer fur of the Black Witch's Ears are recolorable and come with their own unique color, Lydia's Grey.


Inspiration

The Black Witch's Ears were inspried by the design of the YouTuber Zullie the Witch. She first debuted the character that the ears were based on in this YouTube video. but the design for the relic specifically referenced TomatoLover16's renders of the character. The owner, squidbluie, is a big fan of the game design content that Zullie the Witch creates, and wanted the Black Witch's Ears as a tribute.
